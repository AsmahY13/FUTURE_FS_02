# Project Title & Description

**Reformative Stationery & Office Supplies Website Redesign**  
A modern, user-friendly web platform for a leading South African office supplies and technology solutions provider.

## The Business

Reformative Stationery & Office Supplies, a division of Reformative Technologies (Pty) Ltd, is a one-stop shop for office essentials, technology solutions, and business growth support, serving SMEs across South Africa.

Their link: https://reformstat.co.za

## The Problem

The current website is mobile optimized, but its structure and layout feel outdated and somewhat disorganized. Content is dense and the visual hierarchy is unclear, making the site appear busy. While functional, the design does not leverage modern web standards for clarity and ease of use.

## The Solution

The redesigned website delivers a clean, modern interface with improved navigation, clear product/service presentation, and enhanced usability. It streamlines the customer journey, making it easier to explore offerings and get in touch. The new site is fully responsive, loads faster, and provides a more professional and trustworthy impression—directly supporting business growth.

## Key Features & Improvements

- Intuitive, mobile-friendly design for easy access on any device
- Clear product and service categories with improved discoverability
- Streamlined lead management and contact options for faster customer engagement
- Fast, secure, and reliable performance
- Enhanced visual appeal to reflect the brand’s professionalism
- Demo access for clients to explore features easily

**Why is the new site better?**

- Modern, responsive design ensures a seamless experience on all devices, unlike the old site
- Simplified navigation and clear calls-to-action help users find what they need quickly
- Improved speed and security boost user trust and satisfaction
- Professional, clean visuals elevate the brand image and credibility
- Enhanced lead management tools make it easier for the business to connect with and convert customers
